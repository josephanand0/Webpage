COLLEGE HOSTEL

    Our belief that a residential campus offers a superior environment for academics turned into a reality during 2009-10. Excellent and facilitative provision of Hostel accommodation is a major support service offered by CVR College of Engineering. Moving away from home to study is an exciting time in life. There are new friends to be made, new places to discover, new chances to be explored. Every effort has been made at the Hostel to provide the best facilities for students who live on campus. Every student has access to a wide range of activities, facilities and support. The intake of the Hostel was around 340 for the year 2016-17.

    Annual Hostel fees: Rs. 80,000/- (as of July 2019)

    Note: A girl's hostel will also be available on campus on a limited basis from the 2018-19 academic year. The annual hostel fees for girl's hostel will be Rs.73,000/- (as of July 2019).
