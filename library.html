Library

    The College has a dedicated three-storied building with a total area of 1200 Sqm, for the exclusive use of Library and Postgraduate Research facilities. College Bus facility is available up to 6:00 PM on all working days to allow students to spend time in the Library.

    The College library has 79,513 books, 15,830 reference titles and 193 journals that cater to all the eight branches of engineering, as well as other disciplines. A Digital Library has also been set up, with full access to Digital Libraries of IEEE, ASME, ASCE, NDL, DELNET, IESTC, KNIMBUS, NPTEL, Videeya e-books. All major newspapers, news magazines and books of general interest (non-fiction, biographies, etc.) are also made available.

Digital Library

    A number of CDs, audio and videocassettes are also available. The students have access to 20 multimedia PCs.
